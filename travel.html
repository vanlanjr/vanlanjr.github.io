My Adventures

Throughout my life, I have visited many different places, for vacaction and studying, both in the states and abroad.

Study Abroad

For the summer of 2019, I traveled to the UK with a group of students from JMU for a five week trip. We completed two courses, CS 432 Programming Languages and CS 480 Rigidity Theory. The first three weeks of the trip were spent is London. When we weren't in class or studying, we traveled the city and coutryside visiting cool places.

The last two weeks of the trip were spent in Edinburgh, Scotland. After a lot of consideration, I found Scotland to be my favorite destination of the two. The culture, city life, food, resturants, and countryside fascinated me beyond compare.
